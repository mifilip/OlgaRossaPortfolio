About

Olga Rossa is an independent actress, model and singer. Grew up in Aleksandrow, near Bilgoraj in Poland, began acting at the age of 6 by winning a recitation contest. Proud proprietress of natural gold hair and a huge passion, that led her to England. Enormous fan of historical dramas.
